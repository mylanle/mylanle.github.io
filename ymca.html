ymca at virginia tech.

The YMCA at Virginia Tech an independent non-profit organization, is one of the most respected and progressive campus YMCA programs. A large part of this is due to the long-standing and mutually beneficial relationship that exists between the YMCA and Virginia Tech. I had the opportunity to work with YMCA for 2 years as a graphic designer intern, then later on, a Visual Marketing Coordinator. My job was to kketch and design advertisements such as posters, postcards, and webpage banners for future events using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign.

ymca craft fair.

A celebration the beauty and diversity of handmade art at the 47th annual YMCA Craft Fair! Each year, artisans and craftspeople from the New River Valley and eastern United States come together and offer a wide variety of items including jewelry, ceramics, metalwork, glass work, fiber arts, painting, and so much more. Here, I have designed advertisements for this events such as postcards, logo creation, and photography.
