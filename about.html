about me.

Hello! My name is Mylan [mee-lan]. I graduated at Virginia Tech with a B.F.A in Studio Arts, minor in Product Design and acquired a certificate in UX/UI through UC Berkeley Extension Program. I love working collaboratively with a like-minded team to ensure optimal results for user-centered and technology-centered designs. I was previously a full-time graphic designer which as opened up the opportunity to work with many clients' projects and brands such as designing logos, infographics, and digital and printable marketing materials. However, I decided to change paths, and now pursue a career in UX/UI Design. If you would like to learn more about me, feel free to contact me through email or phone number!
